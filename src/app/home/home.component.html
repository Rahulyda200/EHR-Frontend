About Us

Our state-of-the-art, 250-bed facility is dedicated to delivering top-quality healthcare, having served over a million patients to date. Equipped with 100 ICU beds and 15 HDU beds, we provide comprehensive medical services across 30+ specialties, including Neurosciences, Cardiac Sciences, Orthopaedics, Urology, and Mental Health. Our cutting-edge technologies, such as the Da Vinci Robot for precision surgeries and the Mako Robot for orthopedic procedures, ensure excellence in patient care. Supported by a team of 170+ experienced doctors and 450+ trained medical staff, we are a leader in advanced healthcare and medical innovation in the region.

We are supported by a highly skilled team of over 170 expert doctors, many of whom are recognized leaders in their fields, and more than 450 trained medical staff, all of whom are dedicated to ensuring the highest standards of patient care. Our commitment to innovation and excellence is further reflected in our state-of-the-art diagnostic facilities, advanced imaging technologies, and fully integrated electronic health record systems that streamline patient care and ensure real-time access to critical information.

Beyond cutting-edge treatments, our facility takes a holistic, patient-centered approach to healthcare. We focus not only on the clinical aspect but also on the emotional and psychological well-being of our patients. Our hospital offers comprehensive rehabilitation programs, mental health support, and patisrcent education initiatives to promote long-term wellness and recovery.
